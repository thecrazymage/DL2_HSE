Recordings (in Russian): [lecture](https://disk.yandex.ru/d/mzXlT0U3MzEZkQ/%D0%9F%D0%9C%D0%98/DL%202/lecture_08.mp4), [seminar](https://disk.yandex.ru/d/mzXlT0U3MzEZkQ/%D0%9F%D0%9C%D0%98/DL%202/seminar_08.mp4).

## Annotation

In this lecture and seminar, we will continue our exploration of diffusion models. We will introduce the score function and score identity, present classifier ([1](https://arxiv.org/abs/2105.05233), [2](https://arxiv.org/abs/2011.13456)) and [classifier-free](https://arxiv.org/abs/2207.12598) guidance, and derive deterministic sampling ([DDIM](https://arxiv.org/abs/2010.02502) model).
